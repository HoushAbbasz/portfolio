My Onboarding Journey

Key realizations and reflections from my onboarding experience

  • A crucial Professional Development realization that I've made during the onboarding experience was that I need to put more effort than I anticipated to manage my time. I consider myself quite responsible and competent, but I still found myself overwhelmed with the amount of tasks I had to keep track of.

  • A crucial Technical realization that I've made during the onboarding experience has been that web development is more foreign to me than I previously assumed. I have exposure to Mathematical, Statistical, and ML programming predominantly in Python and R but no exposure to Web Development and the programming languages that are associated with the domain.

  • A personal plan that I have for the future is to become more organized. I plan to have three separate notes to keep track of my Professional, Technical, and Personal tasks at the daily and weekly levels. I also plan to establish a routine where I arrive to campus at 8am in order to beat traffic and also allot myself more time to plan out my workload.
